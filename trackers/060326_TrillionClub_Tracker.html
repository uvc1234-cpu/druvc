🎯 The Big Picture — AI Hardware vs. Software Divergence

Hardware wins this cycle: TSM (+4,485), AVGO (+4,048), NVDA (+1,571) — all make the physical infrastructure of AI. Every data center, every LLM training run, every inference chip flows through these three names. UVCMI 777™ confirmed this trend early and has maintained Bull+ signals throughout 2026.

Software mega-caps lag: MSFT (−1,169), META (−660) — despite massive AI investment announcements, the revenue translation has been slower. The BRKB/TSLA anomaly: BRKB (−561) is a non-AI conglomerate in an AI bull market — capital rotated away. TSLA (−455) is struggling with EV margin pressure despite its AI/FSD narrative. Neither has the AI hardware exposure that the market is rewarding in 2026.
