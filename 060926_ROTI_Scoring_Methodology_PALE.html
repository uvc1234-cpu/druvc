§4 — Universe Breadth

The Two Numbers That Drive the GO / NO-GO Decision

The UV Score per symbol is powerful. But for ROTI™ go/no-go decisions, what matters is the universe breadth — how many symbols are confirming across each timeframe.

🎯 The Two ROTI™ Metrics

1. UV Score per symbol (−7 to +7): Identifies leaders and laggards precisely. A symbol at +7 is fully aligned bull across all timeframes. A symbol at −7 is fully aligned bear. Use this to rank and filter.

2. % positive per timeframe across universe: What % of all symbols in the tracker have a +1 for 1H? For 2H? For 4H? This is the breadth signal. Combined with the per-symbol score, it tells you whether the move is broad-based or isolated.

🚨 The GO Signal Threshold

GO: ≥50% of symbols showing +1 in 1H AND 2H AND 4H simultaneously. This means at least half the universe has 3 consecutive green bars in all three short-term timeframes — structural ST convergence confirmed.

WATCH: 25–49% positive. Early recovery signals forming. Monitor for expansion. Do not add aggressively.

NO-GO: <25% positive. Broad weakness confirmed. Capital preservation mode. The dead cat bounce rule: if % positive spikes one day but fails to hold above 25% next session, it was a bounce, not a turn.

Current SOXX example (Jun 9, 11:26am): 1H: 12% · 2H: 9% · 4H: 12%. Deep NO-GO. Only 4 of 33 symbols showing 3 consecutive greens. Dead cat bounce from Jun 8 has fully faded — exactly as UVCMI 777™ predicted.
